Gaudi Parquetry Bed: Queen size four poster bed with side tables. Curly maple, walnut and cherry. The pattern came from a floor I saw in Barcelona in La Pedrera, a building designed by Antonio Gaudi. I liked it so much I sat down on the floor and sketched it. I made the pulls from solid brass. #382
